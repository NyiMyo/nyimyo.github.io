Rice Husk Pellet

Separated from paddy in rice milling, rice husk is considered as waste from rice miller. It weighs around 20% of the total paddy weight. It is abundant in supply and creating a huge problem for disposal for rice milling industry.

Pellet Characteristics

  •  Diameter                               8mm
  • Length                                    10mm to 75mm
  • Moisture Content                 8~12%
  • Calorific Value                      3700~4000 Kcal/kg
  • Ash %                                    10~16%
  • Weight (in buik)                   750~800 Kg/M3 
  • Sulfur                                     0.05%                        

WOOD Pellet

Our wood pellets are derived from the various waste from the forestry industry like saw dust, shaving, veneer waste and off cuts. Most of the species are hardwood form the natural forest managed by Sustainable Forest Management.

Pellet Characteristics

  •  Diameter                             8mm
  • Length                                   10mm to 75mm
  • Moisture Content                8~12%
  • Calorific Value                      4200~5000 Kcal/kg
  • Ash %                                    1~2%
  • Weight (in buik)                   800~850 Kg/M3
  • Sulfur                                     0.02%                        

MIX PELLET

Mix Pellets are made of 50% Rice Husk and 50% Wood Dust. It is meant for some industries with specific needs. We can customize the biomass ration according to the cusomer's request.

Pellet Characteristics

  •  Diameter                                 8mm
  • Length                                      10mm to 75mm
  • Moisture Content                   8~12%
  • Calorific Value                         4100~4500 Kcal/kg
  • Ash %                                       8~10%
  • Weight (in buik)                      750~850 Kg/M3
  • Sulfur                                       0.025%
