Mission & Vision

Our mission is to gain full recognition or acceptance of various type of biomass as a reliable source of energy, alternative to fosil fuel in Myanmar.

With the rapid growth of the Myanmar economy, we strongly believe the biomass shall play a major role to meet the energy demand without significant impact on the enviroment. 
It is our vision to be a leading supplier of biomass fuel in order to support of our clients and society as a whole.

Who we are

*The company was incorporate under the Myanmar Companies Act at January 5th, 2015.
* The first factory was commissioned at September 2015 and the second factory was commissioned at September 2017.
* Our current production capacity is about 21,000 MT of pellet per annum and expected to increase 50% in Year 2019.
